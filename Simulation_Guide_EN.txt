## ðŸ§ª Simulation 2c: Gractal N-Body Cosmology (3D)
**File**: `Simulations/Hubble_Tension/gractal_nbody_simulation.py`
**Linked to**: ðŸ“„ **Preprint 02**

### What does it do?
Generates a "Toy Universe" in 3D with 2000 nodes distributed fractally ($d_H \approx 1.41$).
1.  Calculates the actual density of each point in 3D space.
2.  Applies the "Napkin Logic" to each particle.
3.  **Result**: A 3D map (`p2_fig3_nbody_expansion.png`) where empty filaments glow with high expansion (red) and dense clusters remain stable (blue).
4.  Generates raw data in `gractal_simulation_data.csv`.
